SublimeLoremPixel
=================

Inserts url's for lorempixel.com into html, css, sass and less files.

Usage: 
------------------------
Trigger autocomplete just after **src=** or **url(** attributes. Also typing **http** just after **url(**
will trigger the autocomplete for less and sass.
